ABOUT THE EVENT

Welcome to The Mind Flayer - where reality bends and code comes alive. This isn't just another hackathon; it's a journey into the Upside Down of programming.

Teams from colleges across the region will gather to battle against time, bugs, and the unknown. In 8 intense hours, you'll need to harness your coding powers to create something extraordinary.

Do you have what it takes to survive the Mind Flayer's challenge?

RULES & GUIDELINES

1. Team Formation

Teams can have 2-4 members. Cross-college teams are allowed and encouraged!

2. Original Work

All code must be written during the hackathon. Pre-existing projects are not allowed.

3. Submission

Projects must be submitted via GitHub with a working demo and documentation.

4. Judging Criteria

Innovation (30%), Technical Implementation (25%), Design (25%), Presentation (20%)

5. Code of Conduct

Respect all participants. Harassment or inappropriate behavior will result in disqualification.
